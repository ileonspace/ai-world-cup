# Scoring

The recommended benchmark uses one full-tournament prediction response. Evaluation works gradually as real results become available.

## Group-Stage Match Points

- Exact score: 5 points.
- Correct outcome: 3 points.
- Correct winner: 2 points.
- Correct goal difference: 1 point.

For draws, winner points are not awarded separately because the draw is already represented by the correct outcome.

## Group Standing Points

- Correct group winner: 5 points.
- Correct top two teams: 5 points.
- Correct qualified teams from group: 3 points per team.
- Exact rank: 2 points per team.

## Knockout Points

- Correct team reaches Round of 32: 2 points.
- Correct team reaches Round of 16: 4 points.
- Correct team reaches quarter-final: 6 points.
- Correct team reaches semi-final: 8 points.
- Correct finalist: 12 points.
- Correct champion: 20 points.

## Final Ranking Points

- Champion correct: 20 points.
- Runner-up correct: 10 points.
- Third place correct: 8 points.
- Fourth place correct: 5 points.

## Leaderboard Metrics

- Total tournament points.
- Group-stage points.
- Group-standing points.
- Knockout points.
- Champion prediction.
- Outcome accuracy.
- Exact score accuracy.
- Average confidence.

Calibration metrics can be added later if probability fields are introduced to the response schema.
